## Final Project Proposals! <br> <p style="color:rgb(48, 48, 255)"><font size="+2">Due to my parent's line of work, I have seen some struggles that elders may go through when being assisted into their bed from their wheelchair. The risks involved in standing from one's wheelchair may be too dangerous for some in fragile condition. As a result, one of my proposals is a wheelchair with a lifting seat.</font></p> <style> .center { display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; width: 50%; } </style> <img src="./lifted_wheelchair.jpg" width="400" height="242" alt="lifted-wheelchair" class="center"> <p style="color:black">Image found at <b>https://www.pridemobility.com/jazzy-power-chairs/elevating-power-wheelchairs.asp</b></p> <br> <p style="color:rgb(48, 48, 255)"><font size="+2">However, wheelchairs that have lifting capabilities normally cost thousands of dollars, making them unviable for some in hospitals. In order to demonstrate this mechanism, I would most likely use a smaller model wheelchair.</font></p> <br> <p style="color:rgb(48, 48, 255)"><font size="+2">My second final project proposal came to me when I was moving into my dorm.
